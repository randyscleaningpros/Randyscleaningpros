The 10-Step Gold Standard Procedure

Our structured cleaning procedure helps ensure that every room receives consistent attention. Instead of rushing from task to task, we follow an organized top-to-bottom process designed to reduce missed areas and deliver a more complete result.

Discover the Gold Standard

How We Raise the Standard

Our 10-Step Cleaning Procedure

Each cleaning is adapted to your home and selected service, while following our organized quality-control process.

01

Inspect and Prepare

We review the space, identify priority areas, and prepare the proper supplies before cleaning begins.

02

Remove Clutter Carefully

Light items are handled respectfully so surfaces can be cleaned without unnecessarily reorganizing belongings.

03

Clean From Top to Bottom

Higher surfaces are addressed before lower surfaces to prevent dust from falling onto finished areas.

04

Dust Detailed Areas

We focus on reachable ledges, furniture, fixtures, décor, corners, and other commonly missed areas.

05

Clean and Sanitize

Appropriate surfaces are cleaned using professional methods and products selected for the material.

06

Polish and Detail

Mirrors, fixtures, stainless steel, and finished surfaces receive careful attention for a polished appearance.

07

Vacuum Thoroughly

Carpeted areas and accessible floor edges are vacuumed methodically to collect dust, hair, and debris.

08

Wash Hard Floors

Hard floors are carefully washed using a method suitable for the flooring type and condition.

09

Reset the Room

Furniture and approved items are returned neatly so the room feels comfortable, refreshed, and cared for.

10

Complete a Final Check

We perform a final visual inspection to identify anything that may need attention before the service is completed.
