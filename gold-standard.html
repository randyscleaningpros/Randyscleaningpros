Our Professional Process

The 10-Step Gold Standard

Each visit follows the same dependable foundation, adjusted to the needs of your home, rental, or office.

1

Walkthrough and Preparation

We review the space, identify priority areas, organize our supplies, and prepare to clean safely and efficiently.

2

Declutter and Reset

We carefully straighten accessible areas and move light items as needed so surfaces can be cleaned properly.

3

High Dusting

We begin at the top by addressing reachable ceiling corners, vents, light fixtures, trim, and other elevated surfaces.

4

Surface Dusting

Furniture, shelves, ledges, décor, and accessible surfaces are dusted carefully with attention to detail.

5

Wipe and Sanitize

High-touch areas and washable surfaces are wiped using professional methods and appropriate products.

6

Kitchen Detailing

Counters, sinks, appliance exteriors, cabinet fronts, and other included kitchen surfaces receive detailed attention.

7

Bathroom Cleaning

Toilets, sinks, mirrors, tubs, showers, fixtures, and bathroom surfaces are cleaned and refreshed.

8

Vacuuming

Carpets, rugs, edges, and accessible floor areas are vacuumed using premium professional-style equipment.

9

Floor Washing

Hard floors are carefully washed using methods appropriate for the flooring material and condition.

10

Final Inspection

We complete a final room-by-room review, straighten finishing touches, and check the overall presentation.
