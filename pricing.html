What Your Cleaning Can Include

Your approved estimate will identify which rooms, surfaces and requested services are included.

Standard Cleaning Tasks

  • Top-to-bottom surface dusting
  • Countertops and reachable surfaces wiped
  • Bathroom sinks, tubs, showers and toilets cleaned
  • Kitchen exterior surfaces cleaned
  • Stainless-steel surfaces polished
  • Floors vacuumed and washed
  • Trash removed and liners replaced when provided
  • Beds neatly made when requested

Detailed or Optional Tasks

  • Baseboards and detailed trim cleaning
  • Inside refrigerator and oven cleaning
  • Interior windows and window tracks
  • Heavy grease, soap buildup or neglected areas
  • Move-in and move-out cabinet interiors
  • Laundry, linens or dishwashing by request
  • Heavy pet-hair treatment
  • Customized customer priority areas
