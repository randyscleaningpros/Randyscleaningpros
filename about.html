A Message From the Owner

Hi, I'm Randy.

First, I want to thank you for taking the time to visit my website and learn a little more about me and the reason I started Randy's Cleaning Pros.

I know there are a lot of cleaning companies you could choose. When someone invites me into their home, I understand that they're placing a lot of trust in me. I never want to take that trust for granted.

I wanted to build a company that was known for more than making floors shine or wiping down countertops. I wanted to create something honest, dependable and meaningful—something that could improve people's homes while also helping people in the community.

“I kept asking myself: How can I make a difference in this world?”

At first, I thought, “I'm only one person. What can I really do?” Then I had another thought: maybe I can make a difference.

Maybe I don't have to solve every problem. Maybe I can help one person find a meal, help one family through a difficult week, or help someone without a safe place to stay take one step toward a more stable life.

That thought became an important part of this company and the reason our work means so much to me.

— Randy, Owner of Randy's Cleaning Pros

A Little About My Story

Why This Means So Much to Me

My life hasn't always been easy. I went through difficult experiences growing up, spent time in the foster-care system and faced many challenges while trying to find stability and build a better future.

There were times in my life when I understood what it felt like to struggle, to feel alone and to need someone to care. I've also experienced housing instability and know how quickly life can change when someone doesn't have enough support.

Those experiences stayed with me. They taught me not to judge people by the hardest chapter of their lives. Sometimes a person doesn't need a lecture. They need a meal, a safe place, an opportunity or someone willing to believe they can get back on their feet.

I've had to work hard to rebuild parts of my own life. Because of that, helping people who are struggling isn't just a business idea to me. It's personal.

I want to use what I've been through to create something positive. I want this business to show that hard chapters don't have to be the end of someone's story—and that even a small act of kindness can give someone hope.

What We Stand For

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide dependable, detail-oriented cleaning that gives our customers more time, less stress and a home they feel good coming back to.

At the same time, we want to use the success of our business to help people who may be struggling with hunger, homelessness, housing insecurity or another difficult season in life.

We believe a business should do more than collect payments. It should care about its customers, support its workers and strengthen the community that gives it the opportunity to grow.

We want to leave every home—and our community—a little better than we found it.

How Our Giving Mission Works

From every home we clean, 5% of our earnings will be set aside to help people experiencing hardship or to support a charity serving people in need.

As the business grows, our ability to help can grow with it. Our goal is to turn ordinary cleaning appointments into opportunities to do something kind and meaningful.

Depending on the needs in our community, that support may help with:

🥫 Food or essential supplies for someone having a difficult time
🏠 Local organizations helping people move out of homelessness
🤝 A person or family facing an unexpected hardship
❤️ Trusted community charities doing meaningful work
