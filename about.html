Raising the Standard in Cleaning

Randy's Cleaning Pros was built to raise the standard of what customers should expect from a cleaning company.

We believe cleaning is not just about wiping surfaces or making a room look better. It is about creating a space that feels comfortable, cared for and truly refreshed. It is about giving people more time, less stress and the confidence of knowing their home or business has been cleaned with care.

We are not trying to be the cheapest cleaning company. We are working to be one of the most dependable, detailed and trusted.

Our team follows a structured 10-Step Gold Standard Cleaning Procedure designed to create consistent, top-to-bottom results. We use high-quality products, professional methods and careful attention to detail because our customers deserve more than a rushed, basic cleaning.

We also believe the quality of a cleaning service depends on the people doing the work. That is why we are committed to treating our cleaning professionals with respect, paying them fairly and building a team that takes pride in every home and business we serve.

Every property is different, and every customer has different needs. We take the time to understand those needs and create a personalized cleaning plan instead of using a one-size-fits-all approach.

Most importantly, we stand behind our work. If something does not meet your expectations, we will return and make it right. If you are still not completely satisfied after we have had the opportunity to correct the concern, we will refund 20% of that cleaning service—every time, with no hassle.

At Randy's Cleaning Pros, we are not just cleaning spaces. We are earning trust, building lasting relationships and setting a higher standard—one cleaning at a time.
