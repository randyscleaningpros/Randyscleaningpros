Your Cleaning Helps Create Hope

Randy's Cleaning Pros was created to provide dependable, high-quality cleaning services, but our purpose goes beyond cleaning homes and businesses. We also want our work to help people who are facing hardship and trying to rebuild their lives.

5% of the earnings from every completed cleaning will be set aside to support our charitable mission.

Why This Mission Is Personal

This cause is close to my heart because I understand what it feels like to face difficult circumstances, instability, and moments when the future does not feel certain.

My life has included challenges that required me to keep moving forward even when the road was difficult. I have experienced what it is like to need support, understanding, encouragement, and another opportunity.

Those experiences taught me that people should not be judged only by the hardest chapter of their lives. Sometimes a person needs someone to believe in them, listen to them, and help them find a path forward.

“I want the struggles I have experienced to become a reason someone else receives hope.”

Randy's Cleaning Pros represents more than a business to me. It represents rebuilding, responsibility, personal growth, and the belief that something meaningful can be created from difficult experiences.

Why Giving Back Matters

A clean home can create comfort, peace, and a fresh start. In the same way, compassion and practical support can help a person begin a new chapter.

Our long-term goal is to use part of the success of Randy's Cleaning Pros to help individuals and families who are facing homelessness, recovery challenges, financial hardship, or other difficult life circumstances.

How the 5% Giving Commitment Works

After every completed cleaning, Randy's Cleaning Pros will calculate 5% of the earnings from that cleaning and set it aside for our charitable giving mission.

As the business grows, these funds may be used to support meaningful community needs, including:

  • Basic supplies for people experiencing homelessness or hardship.
  • Cleaning assistance for individuals facing serious life challenges.
  • Recovery, housing, employment, or personal rebuilding resources.
  • Community organizations whose work matches our mission.
  • Future programs created to help people regain stability and hope.
Our commitment to transparency: We will continue developing this giving program responsibly. As donations and projects are completed, we plan to share updates about how the funds are being used and the difference they are making.

When You Hire Us, You Become Part of the Mission

Customers choose Randy's Cleaning Pros because they want a dependable, detailed, and professional cleaning service. But every appointment also helps support a larger purpose.

Your home or business receives our Gold Standard care, while part of the earnings helps create hope for someone who may be struggling to find their next opportunity.

Together, we can clean spaces, build trust, and help people move toward a better future.
