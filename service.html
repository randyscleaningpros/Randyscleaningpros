Our Cleaning Services

Professional Cleaning for Every Stage of Life

Service details may be adjusted based on your selected cleaning plan, property condition, and requested priorities.

01

Recurring Home Cleaning

Weekly, biweekly, or monthly cleaning designed to help maintain a comfortable and consistently clean home.

Commonly Includes

  • Dusting reachable surfaces
  • Cleaning kitchens and bathrooms
  • Vacuuming carpeted areas
  • Washing appropriate hard floors
  • Light straightening and room resetting
  • Final visual inspection
02

Deep Cleaning

A more detailed cleaning for homes that need extra time, care, and attention beyond routine maintenance.

Commonly Includes

  • Detailed top-to-bottom dusting
  • Extra attention to buildup
  • Fixtures, ledges, and detailed surfaces
  • Kitchen and bathroom detailing
  • Floor edges and commonly missed areas
  • Additional time based on condition
03

Move-In and Move-Out Cleaning

Detailed cleaning for empty or mostly empty homes, apartments, rental units, and properties between occupants.

Commonly Includes

  • Kitchen and bathroom cleaning
  • Inside accessible empty cabinets
  • Reachable shelves and closet surfaces
  • Baseboards and trim as selected
  • Vacuuming and floor washing
  • Final room-by-room check
04

Airbnb and Rental Turnover Cleaning

Dependable turnover cleaning designed to prepare a rental property for arriving guests.

Commonly Includes

  • Cleaning guest areas and bathrooms
  • Kitchen surface cleaning
  • Vacuuming and floor care
  • Bed making when linens are provided
  • Light room resetting
  • Reporting visible damage or supply concerns
05

Office and Small Business Cleaning

Professional cleaning for offices, workspaces, and smaller commercial properties that value cleanliness and presentation.

Commonly Includes

  • Dusting office surfaces
  • Cleaning breakrooms and restrooms
  • Vacuuming floors and rugs
  • Washing appropriate hard floors
  • Emptying approved waste containers
  • Cleaning common touch areas
06

Customized Cleaning

A flexible service plan for customers whose property or cleaning priorities do not fit a standard package.

Possible Options

  • Priority-room cleaning
  • One-time cleaning projects
  • Landlord or property-manager cleaning
  • Seasonal cleaning support
  • Specific cleaning task lists
  • Customized recurring schedules
